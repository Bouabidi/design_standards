Circular Processes

System actions/processes begin at a point and end at a point. When a user chooses to create content, they intitiate the action via a button and are then either taken to a form page or are presented with a modal form. In the case of a form page, their expectation is to return to the point of origin once the form action is complete. This is a ciruclar process. Unless supported by specific use cases, All actions must be circular to minimize place/location confusion.
