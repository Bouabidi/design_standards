Community Home

Elements

The community home is an aggregate page with a "home" tab typically containing Web Service controls showing latest activity but limited to the particular community in view. This is also the main access point for the community administrator.

All content within the community tabs must remain in the community context.

  • community title
  • multi-action dropdown button
  • community tabs:
    • home
    • discussion
    • library
    • blogs
    • events
    • members
