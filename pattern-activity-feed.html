Activity Feed

The activity feed is a widget listing both content and social activities of their contacts, their communities and the membership at large. An algorithm, configured by the super admin, and a given user's privacy settings determine the volume and type of content that enters the feed. All content in the feed is subject access controls.

Structure

The activity feed consists of a stack of content. Content blocks have a similiar structure with some additions and subtractions for specific content types. Below is a list of the common structural components:

  • List Filter.
  • Profile picture and name of content creator.
  • Type of content and timestamp of creation/change.
  • Title of the content, linked to the content's context on the site.
  • Community association, if it exists, linked to the community.
  • Content snippet.
  • Tags - if they exist and/or enabled.
  • Action: Remove from Feed. A user control that facilitates a user's desire remove that particular content from the feed.

Feed Content

  • Blogs.
  • Discussion posts.
  • Library entries.
  • Comments.
  • Recommendations.
  • Editorial updates to user created content.
  • Events - creation and registration
  • Volunteer Opportunities - creation and sign up
  • Glossary terms
