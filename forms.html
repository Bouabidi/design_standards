Form Layout

Description

Form control must have labels and they can be layed out one of three ways: horizontally, vertically and vertically centered inline.

Rules & Guidelines

  • Always add labels. Screen readers will have trouble with your forms if you don't include a label for every input. For these input groups, ensure that any additional label or functionality is conveyed to assistive technologies.
  • Do not mix form groups or grid column classes directly with input groups. Instead, nest the input group inside of the form group or grid-related element.
