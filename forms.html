Form Controls

Forms controls are inputs for data on a form.

Guidelines

  • The form control label and the form control should be inside of a form-group.
  • All form labels should be associated with it's form control with the for="controlID" value pair.
  • A placeholder may be used where needed to instruct the user by adding this to the form-control - placeholder="X"

Examples

<select class="form-control">
  <option>1</option>
  <option>2</option>
  <option>3</option>
  <option>4</option>
  <option>5</option>
</select>

<select class="selectpicker" data-width="selectpicker" data-live-search="true" data-width="true">
  <option>1</option>
  <option>2</option>
  <option>3</option>
  <option>4</option>
  <option>5</option>
</select>
