Expand & Collapse

Display widget that utilizes a handful of classes for easy toggle behavior. There are many options with this widget and it's advisable to read the documentation if the task documentation/requirements call for them. below is an example of a stack of expand/collapse containers.

Rules & Guidelines

  • Chevron on right side should move with expand/collapse
