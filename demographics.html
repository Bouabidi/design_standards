Demographics

The demographic control is a multi-function control associating a demographic to a person, volunteer opportunity, speaker and/or mentor. It has two parts, a count and the demographic title and can be configured to show or hide the count.

Elements

  • title
  • 2 part bounding box
    • a count block - increments if "liked"
    • the demographic label
  • link - enabled or disabled
